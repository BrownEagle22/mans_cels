Purple apple tree
Purple apple tree

Going from the Freedom Monument to the University of Latvia along the canal, it is possible to see a very wide range of greenery. When you hear the birds chirping and see a lot of greenery and other bright colors around, it is very easy to forget about the hustle around and immerse yourself in this peaceful fantasy.

Among the more than 100 different plant species, there is also a beautiful purple apple tree. Every season this tree creates a completely different mood, you can't even believe that you are in the same place. In summer it merges with all the other greenery of the canal, but in autumn the foliage already turns reddish-brown and you can enjoy the full autumn dream. In winter, only the twigs remain, not understanding where all this splendor has flashed so quickly, but with the arrival of the spring sun, the richness of the apple tree also returns. Purple flowers bloom, which surpasses even the popular Japanese sakura and allow us to almost fall asleep in these pink dreams for a while.

In the end, I have to wake up from your dreams and keep moving to your destination. However, every day, going to the university, it is possible for a moment to enjoy this flight again through the glorious richness of nature in the heart of Riga.
