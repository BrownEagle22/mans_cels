Akropole

10 years ago, this place was quiet and abandoned. A fenced teritory, in the center of which was the Kuznetsov porcelain factory built almost 200 years ago. Passing by this place, there was an impression that this factory would stand forever.

However something unexpected happened in 2013 - after all this time the whole territory of the factory was demolished, leaving only a huge mountain of rubble. The only thing left were two lonely chimneys. It was planned to build a new shopping center "Akropole" on the outskirts of Kengarags.

Now it is a life-filled place where everyone can spend their free time with family and friends. Starting from shopping for food and clothing to skating and watching movies. Time can fly like wind in this place. If in the past people drove quietly past this place, looking at it as a historical object, then now it is not possible to decide which of the 6 entrances to park the car. Even the south bridge was expanded to allow more people to come here. This place has changed the way no one expected it…
