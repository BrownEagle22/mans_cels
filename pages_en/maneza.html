Sports Arena
Sports Arena

The most athletic building in the suburbs of Moscow. For 55 years, people have been coming here to test their physical boundaries and reach new heights. There are running and jumping, football and basketball, as well as various martial arts. Not so long ago, I was also one of these athletes and could feel the spirit of sport that came from the sports stadium, as well as from the room where we practiced martial arts. It was here that I spent many of the weekday evenings.

Right next to the building there is the large Moscow Garden. One of the largest parks in the area. Great place to regain your breath and relax immediately after sports activities. The garden is especially peaceful in the evenings, when the main garden walkway, which stretches diagonally from the Sports Arena to Maskavas Street, is illuminated in low light. It is a place where you can imagine for a moment that you are out of town and reflect on what you have done during the day. This place is also popular with Nordic walking, probably due to the peaceful atmosphere.
