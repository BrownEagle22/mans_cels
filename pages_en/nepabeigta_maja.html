Unfinished building

Near my home this building cannot ho unnoticed. It has been built for years, dating back to the last century. Initially, it was supposed to be a hospital, but due to historical events, construction stopped 30 years ago and this building became just an object of interest.

As time went on, everything changed. Despite the many summers and winters that this unfinished building and machinery had to endure, construction work continued. 5 years ago, the first changes could be noticed - the structure of the building gradually improved. One by one the floors were placed, one by one the windows were installed. Construction was not fast, but, step by step, changes to the years-old building were already visible.

At this point, all the floors of the building are built and almost all the glass is glazed. It is no longer an abandoned construction site. In a few years, this building will be completed and it will be able to take its place among other buildings. In time, people will no longer know what history this building had and how long it took to build it.
