Freedom Monument

Not far from the University is one of the most important symbols of independence - the Freedom Monument. As you make your way along it, you can watch the freedom fighters and the people displayed at the base of the monument. And in the center, a 20-meter-long obelisk reaching the sky. At the peak of it there is a symbol of freedom of the Latvian people - a young woman holding 3 stars symbolizing 3 regions of Latvia. This place has been through different times and has had different names, but despite this, the monument has survived them, just like the Latvian nation, which stands tall in its freedom.

Freedom must not only be fought, but also protected. That is why every day at the monument there are guards of honor dressed in uniforms with unwavering patriotism and endurance. Regardless of whether it rains or snows, these men are ready to hold the honor of Latvia.

Opposite the monument is the large "freedom square", which everyday is fullfilled with life. On the way to the University, it regularly brings new suprises - with people who perform various compositions and performances, and with various events that are giving the square a new face. This place is the heart of Riga.
