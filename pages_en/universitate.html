University of Latvia

At the end of the road is a building that is one of the most important sources of knowledge and specialists in Latvia - the University of Latvia. It is here that I have decided to devote my time and effort to broadening my view of the world through my studies as well as to expanding my career opportunities. It is also a place where you can meet young people from different specialties and with different experiences. These people include lecturers, course members, as well as study members from other fields.

Studying is not the only activity you can spend time on. Everyone can find a sport, cultural event or other activity they like, in which it is possible to disconnect from studies and have a good time with study members. You can test your skills in one of the basketball and football games, you can test your speaking skills by participating in a debate club and you can do many more other things.

In the end this place ends with a solemn, summer gathering at the front of the university, where people in black robes happily captures the end of their studies. The time will come and I will also be able to enjoy this turning point of life.
